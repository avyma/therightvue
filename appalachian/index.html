Authored by: Vu Nguyen @ New Maps Plus


This commemorative map was created for the Craggy Cartographers - a hiking club for old mappers. Under special permission, a vintage Erwin Raisz map was used as a based map. The map was georeferenced using QGIS 3.10 GDAL Georeferencer. In addition, selective rivers, lakeswater and mountains were digitized with available attributes information. The Coordinate Reference System (CRS) is North America Albers Equal Area Conic (EPSG:102008)

The source of the data are as follows:
  • Raisz_Landforms1957.tif (Copyright Erwin Raisz) - not for distribution
  • Appalachian Trail.shp: Appalachian Trail Conservancy
  • ne_10m_graticules_1.shp: Natural Earth dataset
  • ne_50m_admin_1_states_provinces_lakes: Admin 1 - States, Provinces

Included in the presentation in this webpage are some of the most scenic photographs of Appalachian Trail stretching across the Norteastern states region of Pensylvania, New York, Connecticut, Massachusetts and Vermont. An HTML carousel is designed for this purpose.
